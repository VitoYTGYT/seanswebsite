About Central Illinois Pool Restoration

Your trusted partner for pool restoration and repair since 2008

With over 15 years of experience in the pool industry, Central Illinois Pool Restoration and Repair is your trusted partner for all pool repair and restoration needs. We specialize in comprehensive pool services including resurfacing, tile work, crack repairs, and leak detection.

Our team of certified technicians delivers high-quality workmanship with attention to detail, ensuring your pool looks beautiful and functions perfectly for years to come.
